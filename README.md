# Gender Reveal Bingo Party

A fun, interactive twist on traditional gender reveal parties. Instead of just watching the couple cut the cake or pop the balloon, guests play an active role in the big discovery!

## Project Overview

**Gender Reveal Bingo Party** turns the reveal into a high-stakes social game. It creates a digital bingo experience where every guest is engaged, but with a secret, "rigged" twist.

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

1.  **Guest Input:** Before the game begins, guests select a bingo card (Boy or Girl).
2.  **The Bingo Loop:** As the party progresses, numbers are called out.
3.  **The Rigged Logic:** The logic prioritizes the marking of squares for players whose cards match the true gender of the baby.
4.  **The Winning Moment:** The first person to hit a bingo pattern triggers the final reveal, making the winning moment a shared celebration of their "correct intuition."
